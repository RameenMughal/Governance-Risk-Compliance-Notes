# Incident Response and Business Continuity

- Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan is important in every organization and environment.
- Mostly both Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan are combined in Startups or small company.

## Incident Response Plan

- An Incident Response Plan (IRP) -> ocumented, structured approach that an organization follows to prepare for, detect, respond to, and recover from security incidents, such as cyberattacks, data breaches, malware infections, or system outages.
